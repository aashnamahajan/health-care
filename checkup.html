REGULAR CHECKUP

REGULAR CHECKUPS ARE A MUST.
Make sure that your body is working fine.

Check your body mass index (BMI).
BMI = Weight(kg) / height(m^2)
under 18.5 : under-weight
18.5 - 24.9 : healthy
25 -29.9 : over-weight
above 30 : obese

1 kg = 7000 calories.
If you skip one diet for one month = 21000 cal=3 kg wt loss.
If you skip one diet for one quarter = 63000 cal=9 kg wt loss.
If you skip one diet for six months = 18 kg wt loss.
1 kg = 7000 calories.
6 km walk/day = 350 calories consumed.
If you walk 6 km walk/day for one month = 10500 cal=1.5 kg wt loss.
