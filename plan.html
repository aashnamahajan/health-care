DAILY MEAL PLAN

Early Morning Drink 1/2 glasses of warm water while sitting. Drink 12/14 glasses of purified water daily.
Morning tea Take green or herbal tea with soaked nuts. Almond (8/day), walnut (1/day), pista (5/day).
Breakfast Stuffed roti (1) with curd. Or Cheela-(1/2) (moong dal or besan stuffed with vegetables. Or Oats uttpam (1/2) or Oats khichdi. (1 bowl) or Sweetened Oats (1 bowl) or Sprouts (1 bowl) or Idli /Dokla (3/4) or Khichdi (1 bowl) or 2-3 boiled egg white.
Mid morning Any 2 seasonal fruits.
Lunch Salad, Dal, Sabji, Roti (1-2) & Vegetable Raita.
Evening time Take green or herbal tea with nuts. Or Take a glass of badam (Almond) skimmed milk. Or Any 2 seasonal fruits
Pre dinner Soup or Any 2 seasonal fruits.
Dinner Salad, Roti (1) with vegetable. No rice, protein diet at night.
